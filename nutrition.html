Beetroot Hummus

I love a little bit of a healthy snack! Great with pita flatbreads.

Ingredients
  • 250g cooked beetroot
  • 400g can chickpeas, rinsed &drained
  • 1/2 tsp salt
  • 2 garlic cloves, roughly chopped
  • 2 tablespoon tahini (sesame paste)
  • Juice of 1/2 lemon
  • 100 ml olive oil
  • 2 tsp ground cumin
  • 1 tsp ground black pepper
Instructions
  1. Place all ingredients in a food processor & blend until smooth
  2. Garnish with parsley & a splash of olive oil

Fancy something different? Try swapping out the beetroot.

For a spinach & kale hummus: replace the beetroot with handful of baby spinach, handful of baby kale , handful of parsley leaf

For a roasted pepper hummus: replace the beetroot with 2 roasted red peppers, 1tbs Moroccan spice & 1tsp chilli flakes

Carrot & Chorizo Soup

A bit of comfort food for when the weather takes a turn!

Ingredients
  • 1tbs coconut oil
  • 1 large onion
  • 1 large chorizo sausage diced (save a small bit for the topping)
  • 1 tsp fresh ground coriander
  • 1 tsp smoked paprkia
  • 1 tsp turmeric
  • 1 tsp freshly ground black pepper
  • 1 tsp Himalayan salt
  • 10 carrots, diced/chopped
  • 1 litre water
  • A handful of fresh parsley
Instructions
  1. Place the oil, onion & chorizo in a pot and gently fry
  2. Once the onions have softened, add the rest of the ingredients and gently simmer
  3. When the carrots have softened, take off the stove & blend to desired consistency
  4. Add a sprinkling of chopped parsley & a bit of chorizo to the top

Fancy a bit more spice?

Add extra paprika to the mixture when cooking

Red rice & beetroot risotto

A bit of healthy decadence!

Ingredients
  • 2tbs ollive oil
  • 1 red onion
  • 4 garlic cloves
  • 180g red rice (or brown rice)
  • 2 large beetroots, diced
  • 600ml veggie stock
  • 100g feta cheese
  • salt & pepper to taste
  • Juice of 1/2 lemon
  • A handful of fresh parsley
Instructions
  1. Put the rice in a sieve and rinse it until the water runs clear.
  2. Heat the oil in a large heavy-bottomed saucepan over a medium heat, add the garlic & onion and a pinch of salt and cook for 4-5 mins, or until softened.
  3. Add the beetroot and cook for 1-2 mins, stirring occasionally before adding the rice, coating it in the oil and beetroot juices.
  4. Pour veggie stock over the rice and beetroot, reduce to a gentle simmer and cook with a lid on, slightly ajar, for 40 mins.
  5. Remove the lid when there are 5-10 mins to go. The rice is ready when fully tender with just a little nutty (but not chalky) bite.
  6. Crumble half the feta into the rice and stir through until it melts. Add lemon juice & salt, if necessary.
  7. Serve & sprinkle the remaining feta on top with a bit of parsley.

Good to know!

This ristotto is tasty hot or cold.

Clean Nuts & Chocolate

Healthier chocolate with a fruit & nut twist

Ingredients
  • 350g coconut oil
  • 100g raw cocoa
  • 50g manuka honey
  • 100g crunchy peanut butter
  • 300g mixed nuts
  • a handful of dried cranberries
Instructions
  1. Melt & combine all the ingredients together
  2. Mix well
  3. Put mixture in freezable tray & freeze

Fancy a twist?

Use Almond or Cashew nut butter instead of peanut butter. YUM!
